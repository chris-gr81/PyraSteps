PyraSteps ...better tasking with science

PyraSteps is an app that allows you to assess and manage your personal tasks based on Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs.

Each task you create must be assigned to one of the 5 levels of Maslow's pyramid. In addition to a meaningful title, you can also write down a detailed description or just notes about your task and evaluate the expected scope.

PyraSteps then suggests an order for completing the tasks based on your entries and the assignment in Maslow's pyramid, with which you can probably achieve the greatest possible satisfaction in completing your challenges.

If a task is particularly important to you, you can boost it, which will move it up a few places in the order. And if you ever lose interest in it, just unboost it again.

Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs

Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs is a theory proposed by American psychologist Abraham Maslow in 1943.

This theory outlines a hierarchical model of human needs, depicting the progression of human motivation from basic physiological necessities to higher-order psychological desires. The pyramid consists of five levels, each representing a specific category of needs.

According to Maslow's theory, individuals progress through these needs in a sequential manner, with each level acting as a prerequisite for the one above it. Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs remains a foundational concept in psychology and other fields, offering valuable insights into human motivation and behavior.
